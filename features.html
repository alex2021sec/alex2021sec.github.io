Features

Idea market

Your customers receive uShares™ based on the number of users logging into your product planning market. Individual users may can also earn uShares™ for referring others and, optionally, receive a return on their investments as the idea proceeds through specific stages. Instead of an unending wish-list, an idea market forces customers to spend their limited virtual currency on their real needs.

Stages

Each of your potential improvements is called an investible, and every stage of it's lifecycle is controlled by a powerful state machine. A Uclusion driven business process brings your entire organization in line with customers needs. Customers, engineering, customer success, product management and sales are aware of and control the investible through each stage.

Actionable reporting

An idea market quantifiably represents the health of your customer relationship beyond what other mechanisms can offer. Market overviews and customer speficic drill downs enable you to quickly determine if needs are being met. Data otherwise hidden in a multitude of emails and spreadsheets is converted into a market where all of your customers and product organization participates.

Intelligent end user management

Since your product is the gateway to your idea market, only your customers have uShares™ assigned to them. Our sign on integrations allow your product's account structure to be the same as the team structure that users will be grouped in while investing. To get maximum benefits make Uclusion part of your sign up flow and hear your customers' voice from day one.
