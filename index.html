How we help

Individual customer accounts are issued uShares™, a virtual currency that they can use to invest in a marketplace of ideas to indicate their needs. By making investments users participate directly in the product decision making process and provide honest, quantifiable, real time feedback. With a quick glance you will be able to see the issues most important to your customers, from the entire group, down to a specific customer.

Why is this useful?

Customers, engineers, sales, success, and executives need to agree on priorities. Uclusion allows you to implement a business process that achieves this agreement. Your customers are given their uShares™ backed voice and your engineers are alerted to customer ideas with sufficient uShares™ invested. Market and user team dashboards let success and executives monitor overall and individual customer health. Notification emails tell all stakeholders the stage of their features.
