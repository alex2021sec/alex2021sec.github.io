Use Cases

Customer Success

Development of SaaS products often creates expensive unused features with no return for their investment. Learning what customers want via organic feedback, customer forums or review sites such as G2 Crowd and Capterra happens too late. Similarly, customer advisory boards, up down voting tools, NPS and other surveys fail give customer success teams quick and accurate direction. Uclusion gives customer success the software supported business process they need to track and meet customer needs long before the renewal window. This saves time and churn by having customers organically enter most of the data in a form that makes the intensity of their request clear.

Product Management

Product managers have a full time job worrying about future direction. Usage reports and feedback are expensive ways to determine feature popularity, because they require releasing a prototype and then seeing if customers bite. Uclusion gives product managers a way to get reliable feature feedback from all customers without expensive prototyping. PMs can float an idea in the market, and receive immediate feedback from customers, and sizing information from engineering.

Engineering

Instead of promising features to customers and involving engineers at the last minute, the Uclusion driven process requires engineering input as soon as a feature has enough investment to enter a review stage. This allows both engineering and customers to be fully included in product planning. Instead of an influx of unjustified feature creep that dramatically lowers quality, the entire organization knows which features customers are demanding.

Sales

Uclusion allows sales to grant uShares™ to customers in the pipeline so that their needs can be considered. Uclusion also gives pre-sales a powerful tool for showing new customers how end user driven feature requests are being handled. New customer requirements naturally use the same process that everyone participates in, instead of creating untracked and ungovernable back door obligations. By controlling uShares™ in a Uclusion market you enable the data driven collection of user voice.
