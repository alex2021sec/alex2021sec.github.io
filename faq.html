Frequently Asked Questions

When will Uclusion be generally available?

Free early adoption is available early May, 2019. If interested, please contact us for more information.



I get how existing customers work, but how can Uclusion help sales?

Give new potential customers uShares™ proportional to the needs of existing customers, and know up front what the sale really costs.
By looking at current investment level, the prospect will see what it takes to reach feature implemention.



What if I lose a deal, but I’ve given uShares™ already?

This is a common question, and the first instinct is to “undo” a failed prospect's investment.
We recommend against that because knowing the needs of potential customers is valuable information.



What integrations will Uclusion offer?

As much as possible Uclusion's features are all determined by our product planning market.
However Uclusion is also a user of its own software and looks forward to integrations with Slack and Microsoft Flow.



Is Uclusion hiring?

Uclusion has positions available for summer interns.
We will also be shortly needing various contract work and this FAQ will change from time to time to reflect current needs.



How is Uclusion funded?

Uclusion is currently in bootstrap mode, and not looking for investment.
If you would like to be considered as an investor for the future then please contact us.
