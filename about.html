About Us

Our Mission

Uclusion exists to enfranchise users to direct the growth of the products and services they use.





Our Founders

David Israel

I have over 20 years experience building enterprise software and a passion for customer service. Much of my professional life has been spent fixing bugs on enterprise features that failed to delight customers. Some of those features should not have existed and others should have been better funded. The more dealings I had with end users the more I realized I was building software out of sync with their expectations and that the sheer volume of customers made understanding their collective desires very difficult. Too often we resorted to approximating all customers as being the same as a handful of very large enterprise sales while at the same time not realizing those largest sales had different orgs with different needs. Uclusion was born of a desire to take customer voices to the next level and enable the engineering profession to offer a more rewarding experience. Uclusion is the tool I wish I had 20 years ago.

Ben Follis

I have worked in the industry 20+ years, creating software in small business, government, startup, academic, and large corporate environments. Many of my roles have been a mix of customer facing and pure backend development. As part of the mass migration to the Cloud, I have observed first-hand a continuously widening separation between users and those who develop software. Uclusion is my attempt to close that distance and give others the tools required to development software that best fits their customersâ€™ needs.
